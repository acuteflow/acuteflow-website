1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile applications (“Apps”). Our Apps are designed to run locally on your device with a privacy-first approach. By using our Apps, you agree to the terms outlined here.

2. Apps Covered

This Privacy Policy applies to the following:

  • Game Turn Timer (Android)
  • All future apps published under this developer account unless a specific policy is provided.

3. Information Collection & Use

3.1 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not directly collect, store, or transmit any of the following to our own servers:

  • Personal identity (Names, emails, phone numbers, or accounts).
  • Precise GPS location data.
  • User-generated content or gameplay history.

3.2 Information Collected by Third Parties (Advertising)

To keep our Apps free, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect and process data including:

  • Device Identifiers: Such as the Google Advertising ID.
  • General Location: Inferred from your IP address (used to show ads in the correct language).
  • Technical Data: Device model, OS version, and app performance logs.

This data is handled by Google as described in the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Global Legal Compliance

We recognize privacy rights globally, including the GDPR (Europe), UK GDPR, CCPA (USA), LGPD (Brazil), PIPA (South Korea), and APPI (Japan).

  • Consent: For users in regulated regions, we use a Google-certified Consent Management Platform (CMP) to collect your preferences.
  • Opt-Out: You can reset your Advertising ID via your Android device settings (Settings > Google > Ads).

5. Data Retention

We do not store user data on our servers. Any data collected by Google AdMob is retained according to Google's data retention policies. Because we do not have access to your personal identifiers, we cannot fulfill individual deletion requests for data held by third parties.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our Apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided data through our advertising partner, please contact us.
